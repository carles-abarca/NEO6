      [WHITE]Standard Colors:[/WHITE]
      • [DEFAULT]Default text color[/DEFAULT]
      • [BLUE]Blue - Information and headers[/BLUE]
      • [RED]Red - Errors and alerts[/RED]
      • [PINK]Pink/Magenta - Special emphasis[/PINK]
      • [GREEN]Green - Success and status OK[/GREEN]
      • [TURQUOISE]Turquoise/Cyan - Highlights[/TURQUOISE]
      • [YELLOW]Yellow - Warnings and titles[/YELLOW]
      • [WHITE]White - High contrast text[/WHITE]

      [CYAN]Field Attributes:[/CYAN]
      • Normal intensity text
      • [BRIGHT]Bright/High intensity text[/BRIGHT]
      • [BLINK]Blinking text (if supported)[/BLINK]
      • [UNDERLINE]Underlined text (if supported)[/UNDERLINE]

      [PINK]Combination Examples:[/PINK]
      • [GREEN:BRIGHT]Bright green success message[/GREEN:BRIGHT]
      • [RED:BLINK]Blinking red alert[/RED:BLINK]
      • [BLUE:UNDERLINE]Underlined blue information[/BLUE:UNDERLINE]

      Note: Color support depends on terminal capabilities.
      Modern terminals (c3270, x3270) support full colors.
